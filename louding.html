Victoria Ogega.

Abi Dare's writing in "The girl with the louding voice" is vivid and
draws the reader into Adunni’s world. The novel highlights the hardships women have to
go through starting from an early marriage to systemic inequality yet it also emphasizes
the strength of women and with power of education. Adunni’s broken English can be
challenging at first but it quickly becomes an important part of her character. It's the
story that makes you question the system we are living in cause it highlights every
aspect of female inequality in a way many women can relate to.

Tirsit Berihu

An absolutely inspriring story told in such a beautiful way. Abi Dare
immerses her readers in Adunni's life: her challenges and the various characters that
impact her being. This creates a real view of how the society is, which is calling women
to be brave and fearless to secure a place of importance within the society.
