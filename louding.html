Synopsis

As a third wife in a small Nigerian village,fourteen year old Adunni is expected to fade into silence. But Aduni will not keep quiet.She's funny and curious, with an infectious joyful spirit.Despite her adversity awaiting her at every turn,she's set on getting an education,no matter the cost.

The character in this book not only wants a loud voice but a louding voice.
