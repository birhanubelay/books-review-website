About Saba Book Reading Club

We are seven African women united by our love of reading. Hence "saba" a Swahili word for "seven" and the name of an ancient Ethiopian queen. Saba is a community of bookworms, who believe reading is best enjoyed together. We started in March 2025 in AkiraChix as a space to celebrate African literature and share our experiences as African women.

;

Why We Do the Things We Do?

For us, reading is not just a solitary activity, it's a conversation and we believe that best books spark meaningful discussions and help us see the world in new ways. we created Saba Book Reading Club to capture those conversations and inspire others to join the literary fun.

What Makes Us Unique?

We bring our own distinct African perspectives to every book we read. We analyse characters and plotlines through the lens of our cultural background. We aim to promote a deeper understanding of our shared history and diverse cultures.
